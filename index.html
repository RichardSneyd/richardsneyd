About Richard

With decades of experience in music, Richard Sneyd is a seasoned solo guitarist and vocalist based in Ireland. He has honed his craft to create an unforgettable atmosphere with his unique blend of classic and contemporary music.

His extensive repertoire spans a wide range of genres, from classic rock and pop hits to romantic ballads, ensuring there's something for everyone in the audience. Richard's warm stage presence and musicianship make him the perfect choice for any special occasion. He also performs as part of a duo act, offering an even more dynamic and engaging performance.
