About Richard

Live Acoustic Music for Weddings and Events – Richard Sneyd

If you're looking for a live acoustic singer and guitarist to bring real atmosphere to your wedding or event, Richard Sneyd offers something personal, heartfelt, and beautifully in tune with the moment.

Based in South-East Ireland and available to perform across Leinster, Munster and Connacht—including Wexford, Waterford, Kilkenny, Cork, Galway, Limerick, Dublin and beyond—Richard is a seasoned live performer with over a decade of experience playing at everything from castle weddings and hotel drinks receptions to intimate garden parties and corporate functions.

🎶 Music that Feels Right for the Moment

Richard specialises in creating warm, natural acoustic performances that enhance the setting without overpowering it. Whether it's a wedding ceremony in a country house, a drinks reception at a heritage venue, or a Sunday session in a local pub, he reads the room and adapts to the pace and feel of your day.

His live set blends classic rock, Britpop, modern acoustic hits, traditional Irish music, and heartfelt ballads—featuring artists such as Van Morrison, David Gray, The Beatles, Ed Sheeran, Radiohead, and Pearl Jam, to name just a few. You can expect well-crafted transitions, thoughtful pacing, and a sound that feels both polished and human.

🎸 Tailored Acoustic Music for Every Type of Event

  • Wedding Ceremonies – Walk down the aisle to a live version of a song that means something to you.
  • Drinks Receptions – Keep your guests relaxed and engaged with laid-back, melodic acoustic music while you mingle or take photos.
  • Private Events – Anniversaries, family gatherings and more—music to elevate the moment without being intrusive.
  • Castle & Heritage Weddings – Ideal for Irish castle weddings in places like Kilkea Castle, Waterford Castle, Lough Rynn or Ashford Castle—Richard's acoustic style complements historic spaces with grace and charm.
  • Sunday Pub Sessions – Bring an intimate, soulful feel to weekend events with mellow sets perfect for coffee, brunch or evening drinks.

Richard usually performs as a solo acoustic guitarist and singer, or as part of an acoustic duo for events that need a little extra texture. Amplification and PA is included, with compact setup and minimal fuss.

✅ Trusted, Professional & Easy to Work With

Couples and clients frequently mention Richard's:

  • Calm, reliable presence on the day
  • Fast, clear communication during planning

He takes pride in being someone you can trust to show up early, set up smoothly, and support the flow of your day with music that feels just right.

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

Weekend dates, especially during wedding season (May-September), often book 6-12 months in advance. For weekdays and off-peak dates, 2-3 months' notice is usually sufficient.

What's included in your performance fee?

My standard fee includes performance time, professional PA system, travel within the South East region, and all necessary equipment. Additional fees may apply for travel outside the region or extended performance times.

Can you learn specific songs for our event?

Absolutely! I'm happy to learn up to 3 special requests at no additional charge, provided I receive them at least 4 weeks before your event.

Do you provide background music between sets?

Yes, I can provide background music during breaks at no extra cost. You're also welcome to provide your own playlist if you prefer.
